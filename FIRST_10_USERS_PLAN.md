# LastWish First 10 Users Plan

## Main goal

Get the first 10 real non-Artie users and learn enough from them to sharpen the offer.

## Important truth

At this stage, the biggest risk is not lack of automation.
It is lack of:
- trust
- distribution
- clear positioning
- feedback from real people

So the plan should be **human-first, then automate what works**.

## Target in the next 14 days

- 10 real users try the site
- 3 people complete the workflow or get close
- 3 to 5 real conversations about trust blockers
- at least 1 professional conversation

## Core message to test first

**If something happened to you tomorrow, would your family know where your crypto is or how to access it?**

Support message:
**LastWish helps you create clear crypto inheritance instructions without storing your keys.**

## 3 channels to focus on first

### 1. Founder-led X content
Use short educational posts, not generic promo.

Content buckets:
- family risk / lost crypto problem
- what traditional estate planning misses about crypto
- no-custody / no-seed-phrase trust angle
- stories and scenarios
- professional use cases

### 2. Site trust improvements
Use the site itself as the closer.

Need:
- FAQ
- guide
- professional page
- clearer offer ladder
- sample document or sample pages later

### 3. Direct outreach
Start with warm or semi-warm outreach.

Targets:
- crypto friends and peers
- people who talk about self-custody and inheritance
- estate attorneys curious about crypto
- advisors who serve crypto holders

## Daily plan, first 7 days

### Day 1
- Publish 2 posts: one problem post, one trust post
- Ask 3 people for honest feedback, not compliments
- Track objections

### Day 2
- Publish 1 educational thread/post about what families actually need
- Send 3 outreach notes to professionals or adjacent people
- Refine FAQ from replies

### Day 3
- Publish 1 founder-style post about why this matters
- Ask 3 more people to try the free flow
- Note where they hesitate or stop

### Day 4
- Publish 1 post comparing LastWish vs doing nothing
- Draft one short professional landing page update or partner note
- Collect exact words people use when they describe the problem

### Day 5
- Publish 1 post aimed at spouses / families / executors
- Follow up with prior outreach
- Review which message angle got real interest

### Day 6
- Publish 1 post aimed at attorneys/advisors
- Tighten homepage copy around the best-performing angle
- Ask one user for a short permissioned quote if feedback is strong

### Day 7
- Review the week
- Keep only what showed real signal
- Cut weak message angles
- Plan week 2 around the top one or two angles

## Outreach templates

### Soft user outreach
Hey, I built something for a very real crypto problem: if something happened to you, would your family know where your wallets are or what to do?

It helps create clear inheritance instructions without storing keys.

Would you be open to giving me brutally honest feedback on the flow?

### Professional outreach
Hey, I’m building a tool called LastWish for crypto inheritance instructions.

It is designed to help clients document wallets, assets, and executor guidance without handing custody to a third party.

I’d love your quick take on whether this solves a real gap in crypto estate planning.

## What not to do yet

- do not buy ads yet
- do not automate massive posting yet
- do not spam communities
- do not publish endless feature lists with no emotional hook
- do not assume traffic is the same as traction

## Recommendation

Use a marketing agent as a disciplined operator, not a content firehose.

The mission is:
- get the first users
- learn fast
- turn trust into conversions
- then automate the winning motions
